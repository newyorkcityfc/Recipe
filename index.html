How to make a Heart Shaped Pizza

Prep Time: 10 minutes, Bake Time: 15 minutes, Serving Size: 2 Small Pizza slices

Tools to Make Pizza:

  • Large Bowl
  • Baking Sheet
  • Hand Mixer
  • Cling Wrap
  • Whisk
  • Meausring Cups and Spoons
  • Rolling Pins
  • Pizza Cutter
  • Oil Spray

Safety Precautions: Wash Hands, Wear Mittens, Cook with audult supervision

Cups of Mozzarella Cheese :

Step 1

Add water, sugar, dry yeast, olive oil into a bowl. Mix all the ingredients using a whisk. After whisked, let it all rest for about 10 minutes so that bubbles start to form.

Step 2

Add salt and water into the same bowl and mix it using a hand mixer. Make sure all ingredients are mixed well to form a dough.

Step 3

Spray your hands with oil spray and play with the dough until it is soft and stretchy. Put the dough back into the bowl and cover the bowl with cling wrap. Let the bowl rest in room temperature.

Step 4

Take the dough out of the bowl and lay it out on a light floured surface. Play with the dough and then roll it out into a round shape with 1/2 an inch of thickness. Fold the circle in half and cut it into a half heart shape using a pizza cutter. Then unfold it and it should be a heart.

Step 5

Put the crust onto a greased baking sheet. Then, put the sauce over the crust and sprinkle the cheese onto your pizza

Step 6

Bake the pizza at 475 degrees Farenheit for about 15 minutes until the crust and cheese turn into a golden brown color. Last but not least, enjoy!!!
